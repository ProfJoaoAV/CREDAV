A Busy Day at Greenwood School

It is 10:00 a.m. and the students are studying in their classrooms. In Room 5, the students are having a Maths lesson. They are using their calculators to solve problems. They are working quietly.

Outside, in the playground, Class 6B is playing football because they have P.E. The teacher is shouting loudly because the students are running very fast.

Sarah is in the library. She is reading a history book. She reads well and learns easily. She isn't talking because you must be quiet in the library.

In the Science Lab, Mr. Smith is mixing chemicals carefully. He is showing an experiment to the students. They are watching happily.

I. True or False?

1. The students in Room 5 are studying English.

2. Class 6B is playing basketball.

3. Sarah is talking in the library.

4. Mr. Smith is in the Science Lab.

5. The students in P.E. are running slowly.

II. Interpretation

1. What are students using in Maths?

4. How does Sarah learn?
